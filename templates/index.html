What the confusing words mean

SRT: a subtitle file. If the AI captions are weak, you can upload your own better transcript.

Number of clips: how many final clips you want back from one long video.

Minimum / maximum seconds: how short or long each final clip is allowed to be.

Face track: tries to keep the person centered when turning a wide video into vertical 9:16.

Focus prompt: extra directions like “find arguments, surprises, and funny reactions.”
